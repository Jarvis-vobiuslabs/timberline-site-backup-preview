About our Company

After graduating college at Grand Valley State University     with a double major in FInance/Marketing Richard Lavery moved from Michigan to Dallas in search of a career in banking. As time passed and the banking system continued to deteriorate, the promise of a 9-5 future in a big bank became increasingly unrealistic. While the plan was to move from my current position as a foreman at Pro Mowing to a job that would better utilize my education, it became overwhelmingly apparent  that starting my own business would be a perfect fit for me.


    I pride myself on being detail oriented and this is apparent our companies work. I have cut lawns and worked in landscaping since the age of 15 and work hard to provide the best service in the North Dallas and surrounding areas.

   

    All staff are trained to my standards before they are out servicing our customers without me. At Timberline Lawn & Landscape we understand that your lawn and landscape beds are an investment and are the #1 factor in the way a house looks. That is why we strive for excellence with every customer. Give us a call today and let us show you how good your house can look without breaking the bank!
